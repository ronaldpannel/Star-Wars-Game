Star Wars Game

Humans are fleeing Earth due to climate change, the refugees have
located the Plant Tattooin which can support human life. However,
on the other side of the plant is a Storm Trooper regiment who are
determined to drive the refugees off the plant.

Player Controls: Arrow keys move the Player (Chewie) Up, Down,
Left and Right, F key fires Player Laser

Win Game Conditions: Play long enough for the ship to be filled with
50 refugees. Destroy 20 Darthmaul Clones using the Players Laser
Destroy 100 Storm Troopers by colliding the Player with the
Storm Troopers

Lose Game Conditions : If Darthmaul collides with player,
if 100 refugees are captured by the Storm Troopers
